Quotient Identities:
\begin{equation*} \tan\theta = \dfrac{\sin\theta}{\cos\theta} \qquad \cot\theta = \dfrac{\cos\theta}{\sin\theta} \end{equation*}
Reciprocal Identities:
\begin{equation*} \sec\theta = \dfrac{1}{\cos\theta} \qquad \dfrac{1}{\csc\theta} = \dfrac{1}{\sin\theta} \qquad \cot\theta = \dfrac{1}{\tan\theta} \end{equation*}
